# Revision Engine

Revision is generated from learner history, not from a fixed course calendar.

## Priority Factors

Calculate revision priority from:

- low mastery score;
- repeated mistakes;
- high hint count;
- low confidence;
- long time since last successful attempt;
- prerequisite importance;
- recent deep dives;
- failed project tasks.

## Task Types

- active recall question;
- small implementation;
- explain in own words;
- compare two concepts;
- debug a flawed answer;
- apply concept in a new context;
- resource-grounded retrieval task.

## Revision Rule

Prefer short, frequent revision tasks. Weak concepts should appear more often, but revision should include occasional strong concepts to protect retention.

## After Revision

Update:

- mastery score;
- confidence;
- mistake patterns;
- hint count;
- next review date;
- whether the concept can unblock a dependent topic.
